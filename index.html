Welcome to Roundwood Park School

Thank you for visiting our website which is intended to give you an introduction to our school, graded outstanding by OFSTED since 2009 and affirmed in our most recent inspection (November 2023).

Roundwood Park is a place to flourish! We have a strong reputation as an innovative and exciting place in which to learn and to teach. Our values of kindness, community and excellence shine through in everything we do, whether that is our curriculum, our Aspire programme, our excellent results, our facilities, our smart appearance or our outward-facing charity work.

We hope you enjoy taking a look around our website to find out more!

Alan Henshall
Headteacher
